5 Ingredient Salmon

Finished Product!

Moist and tender salmon with a slightly sweet and salty crust it's the perfect bite!

This recipe is easy and a nice choice to serve for guests who aren't used to eating salmon. Garnish with lemon slices and fresh parsley and serve alongside rice pilaf or roasted sweet potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Nonstick cooking spray
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softned, or more to taste
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons molasses
  • 1 (2.5pound) salmon fillet
  • 1 tablespoon Greek seasoning (such as Cavender's)
  • 1 medium lemon, juiced, divided

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil and spray with nonstick spray.
  2. Stir butter and molasses together in a small bowl.
  3. Lay salmon, skin-side down, on the prepared baking sheet. Spread butter mixture evenly over the salmon. Sprinkle with Greek seasoning and drizzle with Â½ of the lemon juice.
  4. Roast in the preheated oven until salmon flakes easily with a fork, 17 to 20 minutes .
  5. Sprinkle with remaining lemon juice and serve.
